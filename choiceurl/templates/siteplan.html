Overview

Purpose

The internet has milions of website with variety of information. Though it's easy for users to find what they want, remembering the long urls isn't. That is where ChoiceUrl steps in. This website allows a user to enter his/her long url and give it a short name that is easier to remember. The next time the user wants to visit his/her website, he/she can just use the shortname created. It also allows users to give short codes to their files which they can later access in the browser using the codes they created. The website makes use of cookies to keep the codes users created in a browser session.

Audience

The website is free and easily accessible for everyone
